About LoopUp

LoopUp is a private group app for families and close friends that brings planning and fun into one shared space.

Our mission

Make group interaction simple, fun, and worth returning to every week, without forcing people across multiple apps.

What makes LoopUp different

  • Three clear actions: Plan, Play, Share.
  • Private by default for close groups.
  • Built for both coordination and bonding.
