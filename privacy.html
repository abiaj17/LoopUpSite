Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

What we collect

LoopUp may collect account details, group/invite metadata, event content, media uploads, device identifiers, and limited analytics needed to operate and improve the service.

How we use data

We use data to deliver core features (Plan, Play, Share), protect account security, personalize reminders, maintain reliability, and communicate service updates.

Sharing and storage

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by trusted infrastructure providers strictly to run LoopUp. Retention periods depend on account activity and legal requirements.

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of eligible personal data and manage notification permissions from your device and account settings.
